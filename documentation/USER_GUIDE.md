## ‚ùì FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How do I search for properties?**
A: Use the search bar on the homepage or visit the Properties page and use our advanced filters.

**Q: Are property images real?**
A: Yes, all images are actual photos of the properties we represent.

**Q: How current are the listings?**
A: All listings are updated in real-time. Properties are removed when sold.

**Q: Can I schedule a viewing?**
A: Yes, contact the agent through the inquiry form or call them directly.

### Buying Process

**Q: What documents do I need?**
A: Basic KYC documents, proof of income, and address proof.

**Q: How long does the buying process take?**
A: Typically 30-60 days depending on financing and legal requirements.

**Q: Do you help with financing?**
A: We work with multiple banks and can guide you through the financing process.

### Pricing & Negotiation

**Q: Are prices negotiable?**
A: Many properties allow for negotiation. Our agents can help you make competitive offers.

**Q: What fees are involved?**
A: Stamp duty, registration fees, agent commission, and legal fees.
