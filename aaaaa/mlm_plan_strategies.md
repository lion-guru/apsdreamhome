# PRO MLM BUSINESS RULES (APS Dream Home)

### 1. Differential Commission
Rule: (Upline Rank %) - (Downline Rank %)
Site Manager (20%) - Associate (6%) = 14% to Site Manager on Associate's sale.

### 2. Rank Tiers & Targets
- Associate: 6% | Target: 1M
- Sr. Associate: 8% | Target: 3.5M
- BDM: 10% | Target: 7M
- Sr. BDM: 12% | Target: 15M
- Vice President: 15% | Target: 30M
- President: 18% | Target: 50M
- Site Manager: 20% | Target: 100M

### 3. Agent Custom Deal
Feature: Senior agent gets a flat deal (e.g. 5%) and chooses how to split it among their sub-team. Admin creates the deal; Agent manages the sub-ledger.

### 4. Technical Structure
Unilevel unlimited width model. All direct members are connected to the sponsor.
