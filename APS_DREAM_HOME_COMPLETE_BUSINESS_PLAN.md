## 💰 **MLM Commission Plan**

### **Commission Structure (5-Level Deep)**

| Level | Commission Rate | Type | Description |
|-------|------------------|------|-------------|
| **Level 1** | **5.0%** | Direct | Direct sales commission |
| **Level 2** | **3.0%** | Team | First-level downline commission |
| **Level 3** | **2.0%** | Network | Second-level downline commission |
| **Level 4** | **1.0%** | Organization | Third-level downline commission |
| **Level 5** | **0.5%** | Global | Fourth-level downline commission |

### **Commission Distribution Logic**

```php
// Example: ₹10,00,000 Property Sale
Level 1 (Direct): ₹10,00,000 × 5% = ₹50,000
Level 2 (Team): ₹10,00,000 × 3% = ₹30,000  
Level 3 (Network): ₹10,00,000 × 2% = ₹20,000
Level 4 (Organization): ₹10,00,000 × 1% = ₹10,000
Level 5 (Global): ₹10,00,000 × 0.5% = ₹5,000
Total Commission Distributed: ₹1,15,000
```

### **Commission Features**
- **Real-time Calculation**: Instant commission computation
- **Approval Workflow**: Admin approval for commission payouts
- **Payout Management**: Scheduled payment processing
- **Tax Deduction**: TDS and tax compliance
- **Commission Ledger**: Complete transaction history

## 💳 **Payment Plans & EMI Structure**

### **Property Payment Options**

#### **1. Down Payment Plan**
- **Minimum Down Payment**: 20% of property value
- **Remaining Amount**: Financed through EMI
- **Interest Rate**: Competitive market rates
- **Processing Fee**: 1% of loan amount

#### **2. EMI Calculator Integration**
```php
EMI Formula: P × r × (1 + r)^n / ((1 + r)^n - 1)
Where:
P = Principal loan amount
r = Monthly interest rate  
n = Number of monthly installments
```

#### **3. EMI Plans Available**

| Plan Type | Duration | Interest Rate | Features |
|-----------|----------|---------------|----------|
| **Short Term** | 12-24 months | 8.5% | Lower interest, higher EMI |
| **Medium Term** | 36-60 months | 9.5% | Balanced EMI and interest |
| **Long Term** | 84-120 months | 10.5% | Lower EMI, higher interest |
| **Flexible EMI** | Custom | 11.0% | Step-up/step-down options |

#### **4. EMI Management Features**
- **Auto-debit Facility**: Automatic monthly deductions
- **Payment Reminders**: SMS/Email notifications
- **Late Payment Tracking**: Penalty calculation
- **Prepayment Options**: Partial/full prepayment
- **EMI Holiday**: Grace period facility
